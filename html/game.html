Cyberpunk 2077

Description: Cyberpunk 2077 for PC is an open-world, action-adventure RPG where you play as V, a mercenary in the futuristic, dystopian city of Night City, pursuing a unique implant and a path to survival. The game offers extensive character customization, diverse combat styles including firearms, melee, stealth, and hacking, and a story heavily influenced by player choices. Set in a world dominated by megacorporations, gangs, and advanced cybernetics, the player explores Night City's rich, diverse districts, engages in side jobs, and takes on dangerous contracts.
